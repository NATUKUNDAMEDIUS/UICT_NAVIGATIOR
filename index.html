🧭 Navigation
1
Open / Close Sidebar

Tap the ☰ hamburger (top-left of map). Becomes ✕ when open.

2
Detect Location

In 📡 My Location, tap "Detect My Location". Green pulse = GPS active.

3
Enter Start & Destination

Tap the input fields — a full-screen search overlay opens. Type to filter buildings, offices, or rooms.

4
Office Search

Searching for an office shows a 🚪 Office Details card with floor, door, hours and contact — and auto-routes to the building.

5
Live Activities (📋 red button)

Admins post real-time events. Enter admin password to post.

6
Office Manager (🏢 purple button)

Enter password → Dashboard with 3 buttons: View Offices, Add Office, Change Password. Each slides to its own screen with a back button.

7
Reset Map

Tap ⊙ (top-right of map) to return to campus overview.

✅ System Strengths
🚪
Real-Time Office RegistryAdmins manage offices via Firebase — updates visible instantly to all users.
🔍
Office-Aware SearchSearching for an office shows details AND routes to the building automatically.
🧮
Dijkstra PathfindingTrue shortest path through the campus road/walkway graph.
📡
GPS IntegrationUse detected location as start point for first-time visitors.
🔴
Live Activity BoardReal-time events with auto-expiry synced to all users.
📱
Responsive PWAWorks on mobile, installable offline.
⚠️ Limitations
📍
Approximate CoordinatesBuilding positions are manually estimated. Verify with physical GPS.
🚪
No Room-Level RoutingRoutes end at the building entrance only.
🌐
Map Tiles Need InternetSatellite background requires connectivity.
🛰️
GPS ±10–50m AccuracyBrowser geolocation may place you at the wrong building.
